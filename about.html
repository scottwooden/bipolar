HISTORY

Bipolar Studio started back in 2008. It has pioneered multiple powerful VR campaigns. Each short film in our MINI VR campaign collected 1 million youtube views within a week of release.

Soon after, New York Times acquired the films to launch its NYTVR and gave away 1 million Google Cardboards for viewing. The app along with our featured work NYT won the Mobile Grand Prix at Cannes.

Most recently our Google “Daydream” project was set-up as a demo in 10,000 stores across the world to promote Google’s new VR headset. It also comes on every new Google Pixel phone.
